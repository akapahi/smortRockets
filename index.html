Genetic Algorithms

Instructions

Left click and drag to create obstacles. Right click to delete them.

About

Each of these rockets contains a DNA or a set of genes which tells it how to move. Their objective is to get to the target within their lifespan of 400ms. After every 400ms a new generation is created by combining genes from two members from the previous generation. The most fit members have a greater chance to reproduce. Fitness is defined by how close the rocket gets to the target. Slowly over generations they'll learn to get to the target while avoiding obstacles.
